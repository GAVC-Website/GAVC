3D printing

In this class you'll be learning about what 3D printing is and will be able to create and print your very own 3D models and designs to print out. You'll use a variety of applications to accomplish this, you'll also get to learn useful skills such as how to properly operate 3D printers and also how they function. In 3D printing the possibilities for your creativity are endless!
